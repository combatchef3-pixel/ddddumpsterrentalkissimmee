Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a 10-yard dumpster cost on average in the U.S.?

Most customers pay $350–$500, depending on location and debris type. Urban areas like New York or San Francisco may be slightly higher.

Can I get a dumpster delivered the same day?

Yes—in most major cities (e.g., Atlanta, Denver, Miami), same-day delivery is available if you order by 10 AM local time.

What’s the smallest dumpster size available?

We offer 4 yard dumpster rental and 6 yd dumpster options—ideal for minor cleanouts, yard waste, or small renovations.

Do I need a permit for a dumpster on my driveway?

No, if the bin stays on private property. Permits are only required if placed on public streets or sidewalks.

What debris is NOT allowed in roll-off dumpsters?

Hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, tires, batteries, propane tanks, and asbestos are prohibited nationwide.

Weston Lakes is a city in Fort Bend County, Texas, United States, within Greater Houston. Residents voted to incorporate the community in an election held on May 10, 2008. At the time of incorporation, there were about 2,300 residents living in Weston Lakes. The population was 3,853 as of the 2020 census.
